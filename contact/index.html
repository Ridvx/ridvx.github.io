Riduwan

Riduwan is a new media artist, visual jockey (VJ), and motion graphics designer based in Tulungagung. Starting out as a videographer, he has developed his artistic practice through motion graphics, projection mapping, and new media installations. His work often explores local issues through audiovisual and technological approaches. He has participated in various projects with Gulung Tukar and Susur Galur.
